About Us

After seven years of private school teaching, the next chapters setting relocated to an in home preschool. Mountain Top in home preschool was established in 2022, with an exciting future ahead of us all. We love to have kids in our home and watch them as they grow to love learning. The kids enjoy playing games and getting to be outside, they are happy to use their hands when it comes to hands-on activities, but we also love to watch them as they are focused on listening during reading time and during work time.

Our curriculum includes, but is not limited to, The Good and the Beautiful, Right Start Math, Who I Really Am, Joy School, and Charlotte Mason. We find the curriculum we use to help our preschoolers grow and learn to love learning.

Schedule

We live in North-west Boise near Centennial High School. We currently have a class Tuesday and Thursday 9:15-11:45. With enough interest, we are looking to fill an afternoon class from 12:15-2:45 Tuesday and Thursdays as well. Afternoon classes will only open once morning classes are full enough.
